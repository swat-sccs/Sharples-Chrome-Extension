Who is Brandy?

Brandy was a dog that worked at ITS with Oliver, one of our hardware repair specialists. She often visited Kohlberg Coffee Bar on her breaks and was beloved to both ITS and Kohlberg staff.

Unfortunately, she was diagnosed with a terminal bone cancer and was only given weeks to live. She fought for 8 more months before the difficult decision was made by Oliver to relieve her of her pain.

We remember Brandy by calling Kohlberg Coffee Bar, Brandy's Bar.
